Who Am I?

I'm Prince, a Nigerian creative passionate about technology, fitness, and financial growth. I’m currently learning and building as a full-stack developer, penetration tester, videographer, and forex trader, while sharing my journey of self-improvement and balance. My interest in tech started shortly after finishing secondary school, when I realized how exciting it was to understand how computers work. That reflection brought me back, and with God’s help, I’m determined to stay consistent, grow into a career in tech, create impactful content, and inspire others to chase discipline and success in their own lives.
